# Free Mint Campaign

The Free Mint campaign rewards whitelisted community members with the opportunity to mint FOS Digital Collectibles by paying only network gas fees.

---

## Eligibility

**Requirement:** You must complete the [Whitelist](/docs/campaigns/whitelist) first.

Only verified whitelisted users can participate in the Free Mint phase.

---

## How It Works

1. **Complete Whitelist:** Finish all whitelist requirements
2. **Wait for Window:** Free Mint opens after whitelist phase closes
3. **Connect Wallet:** Use the same wallet from your whitelist registration
4. **Mint:** Pay only Base Network gas fees (no NFT price)
5. **Receive NFT:** Your Fos Digital Collectible is sent to your wallet

---

## What You Pay

| Item | Cost |
| :--- | :--- |
| NFT Price | $0 (Free) |
| Gas Fee | ~$0.01 - $0.05 (Base Network) |

**Total Cost:** Network gas only

---

## Benefits

### Same Utility as Paid NFTs
Free mint NFTs have the exact same features and eligibility as regular purchases:
- Eligible for promotional distributions
- Full provenance registration
- Same artwork and metadata
- On-chain ownership verification

### Early Adopter Bonus
First 10 unique wallets in the Free Mint receive upgraded tier allocations.

### Permanent Provenance
Your original minter status is permanently recorded on-chain.

---

## Limits

- **One per wallet:** Each wallet can only participate once
- **Time window:** Free Mint is only available for a limited period
- **First come, first served:** Allocations may be limited

---

## Technical Details

The Free Mint uses a Merkle Tree allowlist system:
- Your wallet address is verified against the whitelist
- Anti-bot protection with cooldown periods
- Transaction signing confirms your identity

For developer details, see [Free Mint Contract](/docs/technical/free-mint-contract).

---

## After Free Mint

Once you have your NFT:
- You're eligible for promotional distributions
- Earn leaderboard points (+2 to +6 depending on tier)
- Join the community as a founding member
