# FOS Campaigns

FOS offers promotional campaigns to reward community participation. Learn about current and upcoming opportunities.

---

## Available Campaigns

### Genesis Campaigns

These campaigns reward early community members:

| Campaign | Status | Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [Whitelist](/docs/campaigns/whitelist) | Active | Social verification |
| [Free Mint](/docs/campaigns/free-mint) | Upcoming | Whitelist completion |

### Growth Campaigns

Promotional incentives based on community growth:

| Campaign | Status | Trigger |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| [Growth Campaign](/docs/campaigns/growth) | Pending | 5,000 active users |

---

## Campaign Timeline

1. **Whitelist Phase** - Join the whitelist through social verification
2. **Free Mint Phase** - Whitelisted users can mint NFTs (gas only)
3. **Public Launch** - Full platform access
4. **Growth Campaign** - Activates at 5,000 users
