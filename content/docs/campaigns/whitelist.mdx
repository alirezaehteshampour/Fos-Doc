# Whitelist Program

The FOS Whitelist is the first step to becoming an early community member. Whitelisted users gain priority access to the Free Mint phase.

---

## What is the Whitelist?

The Whitelist is a verification-based access program that identifies genuine community supporters. It is NOT a financial commitment â€” it's a way to recognize early adopters who believe in the project.

---

## Requirements

Complete these steps to join the Whitelist:

### Step 1: Follow FOS on X (Twitter)
Follow the official FOS account to stay updated on announcements.

### Step 2: Join Discord Server
Become part of the community and engage with other members.

### Step 3: Subscribe to YouTube
Subscribe to catch video content and tutorials.

### Step 4: Join Telegram Group
Get real-time updates and community discussions.

---

## Verification Process

1. Connect your wallet to the FOS platform
2. Link your social accounts via OAuth (secure verification)
3. Complete all four requirements above
4. Your wallet is automatically added to the Whitelist

**Privacy Note:** We use OAuth for minimal verification only. We never store passwords or private social data.

---

## Benefits of Being Whitelisted

- **Priority Access:** First to participate in Free Mint phase
- **Founding Member Status:** Recognized as an early supporter
- **Upgraded Tiers:** First 10 unique wallets receive tier upgrades
- **Community Recognition:** Listed as a genesis community member

---

## Timeline

The Whitelist phase runs during the community building period. Once closed, no new entries are accepted.

**Status:** Check the [Roadmap](/docs/roadmap) for current phase.

---

## Next Steps

After completing the Whitelist:

1. Wait for the Free Mint window to open
2. Proceed to the [Free Mint Campaign](/docs/campaigns/free-mint)
