### Why Retail Adopts Base

1.  **Distribution**: 100M+ verified Coinbase users via Smart Wallets.
2.  **Economic Viability**: Sub-cent fees enable micro-transactions.
3.  **Culture**: Home of "Onchain Culture" (Zora, Blackbird).

## Strategic Synthesis

The convergence of massive TAM growth in digital collectibles ($50B+), the demand for efficient rewards ($37B+ affiliate market), and Base's infrastructure maturity creates a high-conviction opportunity for **Automated Reward Ecosystems**.
